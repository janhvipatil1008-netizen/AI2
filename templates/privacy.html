Privacy

AI² is a learning platform in private beta. This page explains the current data handling approach in clear language; it is not a final legal policy for public launch.

What AI² May Store

AI² may collect account information such as your email address and display name if authentication is enabled.

The platform stores learning progress, todos, topic notes, quiz submissions, portfolio submissions, interview submissions, AI feedback, usage events, learning outcome scores, and beta feedback.

AI Provider Use

AI² uses Claude/Anthropic integration to generate and evaluate learning content. When you request AI feedback, your submissions may be sent to the AI provider so the platform can generate that feedback.

Debug And Health Views

Debug and health views are protected in production and should not expose full private content. AI² avoids exposing secrets, database URLs, API keys, raw environment values, or full private learner content in debug surfaces.

Beta Requests

During private beta, users can request deletion or export manually through the project owner or support contact. Data handling may evolve before public launch as the product and storage model mature.
