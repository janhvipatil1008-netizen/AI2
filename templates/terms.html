Terms

AI² is provided as a private beta learning and practice support platform. Features may change as the product is tested with early learners.

Learning Support

AI² helps learners practice topics, quizzes, portfolio work, interviews, reflections, todos, and learning outcomes. AI feedback may be imperfect and should not be treated as guaranteed professional, legal, career, academic, or financial advice.

User Responsibilities

Please do not submit sensitive personal information, private credentials, financial information, health information, or confidential third-party material. Use the platform respectfully and only for learning, practice, and feedback.

Limits And Abuse

Abuse, scraping, automated harvesting, attempts to bypass limits, or attempts to disrupt the service are not allowed. Free trial limits may apply, including limits on AI actions or other beta features.

Beta Changes

Beta features may change, pause, or be removed. Paid plans may be introduced later after private beta signals are reviewed.

Contact

For support, privacy requests, deletion/export requests, or beta feedback, contact the AI² project owner. A dedicated support address will be added before public launch.
